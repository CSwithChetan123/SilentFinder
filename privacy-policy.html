1. Introduction

SilentFinder is designed to help users locate their phone by triggering a loud ring and flashlight even when the phone is in silent mode ‚Äî using a predefined SMS keyword. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

2. Data Collection and Use

SilentFinder does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No user data is transmitted to our servers.
  • No location, contacts, or personal information is collected.
  • The app works entirely offline and uses only the SMS received by your device.

3. Permissions Used

  • SMS Permission: To detect incoming messages that match your saved keyword. SMS is only processed locally.
  • Do Not Disturb / Audio Control: To override silent mode and ring the phone.
  • Flashlight Permission: To blink the flashlight during phone ringing.

These permissions are used only for the app's core functionality.

4. Android 14 and Above

Due to changes in Android's background service restrictions, SilentFinder may not function as expected on Android 14 and higher unless you manually allow background operations and grant all necessary permissions.
